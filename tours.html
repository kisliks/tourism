Quick Itineraries

Perfect plans for a weekend

🇮🇹 Italy: Classic Weekend

    Start in Rome to see the Colosseum, then take a fast train to Florence for Renaissance art and world-class food. Finish your journey in Venice, wandering through its iconic canals and historic bridges. For a coastal bonus, add a quick trip to the colorful villages of the Amalfi Coast or Cinque Terre.

🇯🇵 Japan: Tradition & Future

    Start in Tokyo to experience a mix of futuristic neon streets and traditional shrines. Take the bullet train to Kyoto to explore stunning golden temples and authentic geisha districts. Finish in Osaka to enjoy incredible street food and the historic beauty of its famous castle.
Book This Plan

🇮🇸 Iceland: Fire & Ice

    Start in Reykjavik to explore the Golden Circle’s powerful geysers and the dramatic Gullfoss waterfall. Drive along the South Coast to witness the stunning black sand beaches of Vik and the Jökulsárlón glacier lagoon. End your adventure with a soak in the geothermal waters of the Blue Lagoon before heading home.
